Our Story

Brewing Peace Since 2024

The Beginning

Bloom & Breeze was born out of a simple desire: to create a space where the frantic pace of the world slows down. We believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just caffeine; it's a moment of reflection, a pause in the day, and a breath of fresh air.

Starting as a small botanical stall in Bloom City, our founders envisioned a sanctuary that combined their love for rare coffee beans and exotic greenery.

Our Mission

We source our beans ethically from high-altitude farms, ensuring every sip supports sustainable practices. Our café is more than a business; it's a community where art, nature, and the craft of coffee converge.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with a friend, or simply watch the sunlight filter through our leaves, we welcome you to Bloom & Breeze.

A botanical sanctuary for coffee lovers.
